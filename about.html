About Our Website

Welcome to RentMyCaravan!

This is a prototype website designed to test our skills on website development.
On this website, you can view listings of rental caravans.
Each caravan has a listing that is displayed in a grid.
These listings contain information for:

  • Caravan Descriptions
  • Location of the caravan
  • External links for images and videos
  • Price per night to occupy the caravan
  • Date ranges for availability
  • Factilities and amenities that the caravan has, such as showers and kitchens
  • Additional information and details

Each listing can also be edited, deleted, viewed for summary with more specific information.
You can also log in, log out, view reviews.
